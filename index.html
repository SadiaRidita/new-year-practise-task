My failures of previous year

I used to think that “success” in life meant living every moment in a way that I would not later regret. How ironic it’s been as I look back on my life and have too many regrets to count!

  • Not meeting others' expectations.
  • Missing a deadline.
  • Taking on too much/over-promising.
  • Failing an assignment.
  • Not getting a job.
